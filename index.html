Hey, I'm Ross Rudolph. I make things in order to cope and get closer to god through nature, which is currently coming out through terrariums. Here in sf, I work with the land while studying horticulture and serving public good at Alemany Farm, the Conservatory of Flowers, and the Jung Institute. I explore how tech can be used for ritual through my interactive pieces 'god image' and 'trance decay,' which exhibited at Gray Area. Deeply disturbed by the state of our society, I have stepped away from participating in product design for now.
